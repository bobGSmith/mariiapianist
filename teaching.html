Teaching

Lessons are structured, supportive, and tailored to each student — with clear goals, healthy technique, and confident musical expression.

What lessons cover

  • Technical foundations and relaxed, efficient movement
  • Sound, tone, phrasing, articulation, and pedalling
  • Rhythm, reading skills, and musical independence
  • Repertoire planning and effective practice methods
  • Performance preparation and stage confidence

Lesson formats

  • In-person (London) or online
  • 30 / 45 / 60 minutes
  • Weekly lessons or one-off coaching sessions
  • Optional support for exams, auditions, and competitions

Who it’s for

Mariia teaches a wide range of students and adapts the approach to suit age, level, and goals.

  • Children and teenagers — building strong fundamentals and musical enjoyment
  • Beginners — starting confidently with clear, step-by-step progression
  • Adult beginners — supportive coaching and efficient practice strategies
  • Intermediate and advanced — refining technique, tone, and interpretation
  • Audition & exam preparation — structured planning and performance readiness
  • Returning players — rebuilding confidence and technique after time away
